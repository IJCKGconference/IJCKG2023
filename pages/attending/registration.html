The IJCKG registration site is hosted on Peatix.com, an event ticketing service.

  2. Then, go to https://ijckg2023.peatix.com/view and register.

Note

  • Online participation is free of charge.
  • An additional fee of JPY 3,000 will be charged for presentations at the main conference and poster sessions, in addition to the participation fee.
  • The poster sessions will be held on-site only. (The poster presentations will not be streamed online.)
