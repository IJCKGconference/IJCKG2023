The 12th International Joint Conference on Knowledge Graphs (IJCKG 2023) is a premium academic forum on Knowledge Graphs. The mission of IJCKG 2023 is to bring together international researchers in the Knowledge Graph community and other related areas to present their innovative research results or novel applications of Knowledge Graphs.

IJCKG2023 will take place from December 8 to 9, 2023 in Miraikan - The National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation, Tokyo, Japan. IJCKG2023 will be a hybrid conference, with both in-person and virtual attendance options.
